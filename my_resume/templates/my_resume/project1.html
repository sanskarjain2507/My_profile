PROJECT:Book store



Description:


This is an e-commerce site for books.In this website the entities are
  1. Admin
  2. Customer
  3. Employee

Admin Roles:

  • Add and delete Employees
  • View his profile
  • Update his profile
  • View Orders
  • View Feedbacks
  • Change password
  • View employess data
  • Search for any product
  • Logout

Customer Roles:

  • Signup
  • Change password
  • View Full Info about any product
  • Add a product to his cart
  • Buy a product
  • Search for any product based on various criteria
  • Submit Feedback
  • Change his profile details
  • log out

Employee Roles:

  • Add a product
  • View orders
  • Confirm order Shipping
  • View feedbacks
  • View delivered orders
  • Update or delete any product
  • Logout
